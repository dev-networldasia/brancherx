    Make customer choose your brand because of functional benefits, and engage to your brand because of collective benefits.

    Functional Benefits

    Rational benefits focused on core product and service deliverables: technology, functionality, price, support and ease of customer interactivity.

    Personal Benefits

    How the business delivers benefits from within the category that serve a specific personal need or want and touch the hearts and minds of your audience.

    Collective Benefits

    How you bring benefits to the consumer beyond the category. Enabling and facilitating added value, very often tied to a bigger social or cultural narrative.
